Delicious Tunisian Bites Menu

Appetizers

  • Brik: Crispy pastry filled with egg, tuna, and capers, deep-fried to perfection.
  • Salade Mechouia: A grilled vegetable salad with tomatoes, peppers, onions, and a tangy lemon dressing.
  • Chakchouka: A flavorful stew of tomatoes, peppers, and eggs, spiced with cumin and paprika.

Main Courses

  • Couscous: Traditional semolina dish served with a rich stew of lamb, vegetables, and chickpeas.
  • Tagine: Slow-cooked stew with chicken, olives, and preserved lemons, served with crusty bread.

Desserts

  • Makroud: Semolina cakes filled with dates and nuts, deep-fried and coated in honey.
  • Zgougou: A sweet pudding made from pine nuts and flavored with rose water.
  • Baklawa: Layers of filo pastry filled with nuts and sweetened with honey syrup.
